Dolphinately: Translator’s Interactive Training Hub
A smart platform for building real translation skills.
This innovative tool is designed for translators at all stages—students, fresh graduates, junior translators, and seasoned professionals—offering a rich and engaging learning experience in both English and Arabic.
The platform features a vast collection of interactive exercises covering key translation competencies and organized by skill level—from beginner to advanced. Whether you're sharpening grammar, enhancing terminology, or improving localization techniques, each exercise is tailored to help you grow.
With real-time feedback on incorrect answers, users can learn from their mistakes and track their improvement over time. Progress is saved automatically, allowing users to pause and resume anytime.
To keep motivation high, users earn digital badges upon completing key levels, which can be proudly shared on platforms like LinkedIn to showcase skills and commitment.
What sets this tool apart is its library of specialized glossaries, complete with localization notes, making it not only a perfect fit for individual learners but also a valuable asset for translation agencies and localization teams seeking high-quality, domain-specific terminology.
Whether you're preparing for a job, aiming to improve your performance, or simply passionate about translation—this tool is your go-to companion for practical, structured, and rewarding training.
Why Dolphinately?
✔️ Targeted Learning: Exercises are categorized by specific translation competencies and levels, so you focus exactly on what you need to improve.
✔️ Instant Feedback: Get immediate, helpful feedback on your answers to learn and correct mistakes on the spot.
✔️ Progress Tracking: Save your progress, pick up where you left off, and watch your growth over time.
✔️ Recognition: Earn shareable badges as you complete levels—great for boosting your LinkedIn profile and showcasing your development.
✔️ Specialized Glossaries: Access curated, localized glossaries that support both learners and companies looking to improve translation quality and consistency.
✔️ Bilingual Practice: Work in both English and Arabic to build real-world translation skills in both directions.
Features That Set Us Apart
🔹 Bilingual & Interactive
Practice translation in both English and Arabic through rich, interactive exercises designed to simulate real-world translation challenges.
🔹 Competency-Based Learning Paths
Exercises are organized by competency and skill level, allowing users to build specific strengths—grammar, terminology, localization, editing, and more.
🔹 Smart Feedback System
Our tool provides instant, constructive feedback on wrong answers to help users understand, reflect, and improve—turning every mistake into a learning opportunity.
🔹 Progress Tracking & Save Feature
Your learning journey is fully trackable. You can save your progress, return to unfinished tasks, and monitor your development over time.
🔹 Gamified Learning with Shareable Badges
Earn achievement badges after completing levels and share them on platforms like LinkedIn to celebrate your growth and gain visibility.
🔹 Specialized, Localized Glossaries
Access an expanding collection of industry-specific glossaries with proper localization—ideal for learners and also highly valuable for companies and localization teams.
🔹 Designed for All Levels
From students and fresh graduates to senior professionals, our platform adapts to every learner’s pace and experience level.
